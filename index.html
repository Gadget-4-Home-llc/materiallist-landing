Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Identity

This service is operated by Gadget 4 Home LLC, a company registered in the United States.

2. Minimal Data Collection

We only collect your email address for authentication (Login) purposes and to secure your account. We do not collect, store, or have access to your passwords, browsing history, or personal data from websites you visit.

3. AI Privacy

Material List does not process your data with internal AI. The extension provides a pre-built prompt that you can use with your own ChatGPT account. Your interaction with OpenAI remains entirely private and under your control. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the AI's responses.

4. Cloud Storage

Your data — including material lists, company logos, client information, and settings — is stored securely and encrypted on Supabase (hosted on AWS) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. This ensures that only you can access your own data. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

5. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, distribute, or share your data with third parties for any purpose. Your business information remains yours.

6. Payment Processing

Subscription payments are handled securely by Stripe. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card or payment information.

7. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support@gadget4home.com. We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days of your request.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication and encrypted data storage
  • Stripe — Secure payment processing
  • Google Chrome Web Store — Extension distribution

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Material List after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
